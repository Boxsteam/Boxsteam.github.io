About Me

Justin Norman has earned experience and many accomplishments involved in the field of Computer Science as well as Game Design. This took off in high school when he entered the FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) Robotics Program which helped motivate and inspire Justin to pursue Computer Science and helped further his future goal of heading into Game Design. He is currently enrolled in the University of Houston-Victoria on the pursuit to achieve his second degree which is a Bachelor's Degree in Digital Gaming & Simulation.
